HighLytr

HighLytr is a groundbreaking platform designed to empower high school athletes, with a focus on those
outside the United States, by providing them with a global platform to showcase their talents, connect
with college coaches, and secure scholarships to pursue their athletic and academic dreams. We
believe that talent knows no boundaries, and HighLytr seeks to level the playing field by connecting
athletes from diverse backgrounds with college opportunities.

We are a team of five graduate students from the Master of Science in Entrepreneurship and Innovation
(MSEI) program at the University of San Francisco. Our backgrounds and experiences span various
disciplines, bringing a diverse set of skills to the table. Together, we are passionate about innovation
and making a positive impact.
